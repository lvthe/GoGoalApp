What is the Hayya World Cup?

Is the Hayya World Cup a gamble?

No. We provide a free to play platform and reward for the top predictions from our marketing wallets. You don’t need to pay anything to play.

What kinds of predictions do Hayya World Cup support?

The current version supports classic game outcome prediction. In future versions, HAYYA World Cup will support more complex prediction modes such as specific scores, ranking prediction as well as live prediction.

You DON'T need to spend hundreds of dollars to purchase an NFT. If you are an early $HYC holder, just pay the gas fee to get a cute and fun Hayya World Cup NFT. Hold Hayya World Cup NFT for in-game perks such as getting more rewards, participation in VIP prediction games and more.

We charge a very high tax at the listing time so that bots cannot purchase the token at the floor price.
